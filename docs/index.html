Vocal chain

Six stages, one focused vocal workflow.

Sori 1 is built around the vocal processing order engineers actually reach for: clean harshness, control resonance, stabilize level, shape tone, add density, then finish with size.

01

DeEss

Split-band sibilance control designed to tame consonants without collapsing air.

02

Res EQ

Dynamic resonance suppression that listens for narrow build-up around the target area.

03

Comp

Vocal-first leveling with peak and RMS detection for stable frontness.

04

EQ

Body, focus, presence, and air controls mapped for fast vocal tone decisions.

05

Sat

Soft harmonic density for warmth, edge, and presence without brittle gain.

06

Inflate

Final perceived size, width, blend, and output control for a finished vocal.

Stage-aware assistant

Ask the part of the chain you are working on.

The assistant reads the selected stage, then maps requests to the right controls. A harsh vocal prompt on DeEss is treated differently from the same prompt on EQ or Saturation.
